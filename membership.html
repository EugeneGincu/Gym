Membership Tiers

Divs with membership tiers, prices and benefits, for different age groups floated with wrap for mobile.

Form to sign up for membership.

Bronze

The perfect starting point. Bronze gives you everything you need to build a consistent routine and get comfortable in the gym.
  • Includes:
  • Weight-lifting floor access (unlimited)
  • Sign up for Fitness & Cardio classes
  • Book the Fitness or Cardio coach (1-on-1)

Silver

More access, more variety, more results. Silver is built for members who want to explore everything the gym has to offer and start working with expert coaches. Includes everything in Bronze, plus: ✅ Sign up for all classes (Spin, Fitness, Stretching, Cardio) ✅ Book any coach (Nutritionist, Cardio, Fitness, Strength, Stretching, Weight Loss, Spin) ✅ Book rooms for your own group classes (Fitness & Cardio rooms) ✅ Free anytime access to Fitness & Cardio rooms
